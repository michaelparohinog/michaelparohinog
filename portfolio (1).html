Case Studies

A closer look at how each partnership solved a real client problem — pulled from the ventures above.

01 — PLASTIC PACKAGING / COMMUNITY MARKETING MATERIALS
PROBLEM

The client needed a reliable and cost-effective supplier for community marketing materials but lacked technical expertise in selecting the right banner specifications.

SOLUTION

We recommended the appropriate banner size, thickness, and material based on the application, offered competitive pricing, and ensured fast production and delivery.

OUTCOME

Reduced procurement costs, faster campaign deployment, improved material quality, and a reliable long-term supply partner.

02 — METAL WORKS & GENERAL TRADING / METAL WORKS
PROBLEM

The client required custom-fabricated metal products but struggled to find a supplier that could meet their specifications, quality standards, and project deadlines.

SOLUTION

We designed and fabricated customized metal solutions tailored to their operational requirements, from concept to production.

OUTCOME

Improved operational efficiency, minimized project delays, and delivered durable, high-quality metal solutions.

02 — METAL WORKS & GENERAL TRADING / CUSTOM MACHINERY & INDUSTRIAL SUPPLIES
PROBLEM

The client needed specialized machinery and a dependable supplier for industrial and operational requirements.

SOLUTION

We designed and manufactured a custom-built machine based on the client's workflow while also supplying the necessary industrial materials and general supplies.

OUTCOME

Increased productivity, streamlined operations, reduced manual work, and established a reliable long-term procurement partner.

03 — SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT / BUDGET TRACKING APP
PROBLEM

Many businesses and consumers lacked an intuitive and reliable way to track budgets and manage their finances.

SOLUTION

We developed a user-friendly budget tracking application focused on simplicity, performance, and user experience.

OUTCOME

Achieved the #1 Paid Finance App ranking on both the Google Play Store and Apple App Store, demonstrating strong user adoption and market demand.
